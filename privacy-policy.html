Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube to TikTok Automation ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service to post content to TikTok.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect:

  • TikTok Account Information: Basic profile information (username, display name) provided through TikTok's OAuth authentication
  • Video Content: Videos you choose to upload through our service
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our App

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Post videos to your TikTok account on your behalf
  • Improve our service

4. Data Storage

Your data is processed locally on your own computer/server. We do not store your videos, TikTok credentials, or personal information on external servers. Authentication tokens are stored locally and securely.

5. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with:

  • TikTok API: To post content to your account. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • YouTube: To download publicly available content for processing

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your content is only shared with TikTok when you explicitly choose to post it.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All API communications use HTTPS encryption. Access tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your TikTok account at any time
  • Request deletion of any locally stored data
  • Revoke app permissions through TikTok's settings

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

11. Contact Us
